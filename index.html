Rocky Python

A Denver-Boulder area special interest group connecting people with interests in web development, data science, biology, and anything related to the Python programming language, at all levels of expertise.

Sponsors

If you or your business would like to sponsor our groups by providing food, venue, or giveaways, please email us at rockypython@googlegroups.com

Join us on Discord

Meetup events aren't the only time for chatting. There's also a Discord server. Sign on to ask questions, give answers, or just hang out. All group channels are available at https://discord.gg/7RXCGwxAzw. Discord is great place to promote a job posting at your company.

Upcoming meetups

See the respective group page from the site menu. Study and co-working events are generally hosted at the same place and time for all groups. We are currently looking for venues to host monthly meetups where members and speakers can give talks. If you or your business would like to host a monthly meetup, please contact us at rockypython@googlegroups.com.
